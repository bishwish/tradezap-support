FAQ

How do I track a trade?

Tap the + button on the home screen, fill in the trade details, and save.

How do I view my performance?

Go to the Statistics tab to see your P/L charts and analytics.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings > Subscription to manage your subscription.
